# Synthetic Participant Policy

Kiby includes synthetic participants to create a healthy baseline social environment and leaderboard activity for early players.

## Disclosure
- Synthetic participant use is disclosed in project documentation and operator policy.
- Synthetic profiles are designed to simulate routine pet-care behavior.

## Product Intent
- Improve launch-day community feel and competition density.
- Support progression pacing while real player population grows.

## Guardrails
- Synthetic participation is limited to game simulation behavior.
- Synthetic participants do not receive privileged gameplay mechanics.
- Competitive systems should remain balanced for real players.
